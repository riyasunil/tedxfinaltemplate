OUR THEME

Metamorphosis

Every triumph is a tale of constant adaption, revision and change. Progress is impossible without change, which in turn leads us through the path of evolution to what we make of ourselves down the road.

Our way of perception, here at MEC does not go with the enormous hype that the world gives to the destination and all that it brings. While success is cardinal, it's the beauty of the journey that should be celebrated. To give an eye of detail to every change around us is to embrace the metamorphosis.

While we are at it, let’s dance to the beats of changes and blow out of the bubble, embracing the little episodes along the way!

Join us as we delve into the world of metamorphosis and discover its transformative power in technology, entertainment, and design.

Meet Our Speakers

Aisha Nazia

Aisha Nazia is a sports management expert with an impressive track record of managing high-profile sporting events such as the FIFA Under-17 World Cup, National Games, Indian Super League, and NBA India Games. Her unwavering commitment to exce-Ilence and vision for inclusive and innovative sports practices is truly transformative. Aisha aspires to use her knowledge and experience to elevate the Indian sports ecosystem. She was also fortunate enough to fulfill her dream of being part of the F1 Grand Prix experience at Silverstone Circuits Ltd. Aisha is a true inspiration for women breaking barriers in the sports industry.

Manas Madhu

Manas Madhu is an Indian entrepreneur and co-founder of Beyond Snack. He pitched his company in the first season of Shark Tank India and was the first entrepreneur to close a deal with not one, but two sharks. Beyond Snack provides healthy snack options made from natural ingredients. Manas is passionate about promoting entrepreneurship and sustainability and has spoken at several events on these topics.

Siddhi Mahajankatti

Siddhi Mahajankatti is an actor, dancer, anchor, and data scientist known for her talent and passion in the entertainment industry. She made her debut in 2015 with the play Charandas Chor and gained nationwide fame in 2016 with her performance in the Malayalam film Aanandham. She has also appeared in web series and talk shows and is currently working on an untitled Tamil feature film. Siddhi's talent, passion, and dedication make her a versatile and sought-after performer.

'Khyrunnisa A

Khyrunnisa A. is an award-winning writer of children's fiction and adults. She created the comic character Butterfingers and authored the Butterfingers series of books for older children. This character first made his appearance in the Indian children's magazine,Tinkle. She has written two books for adults, two collections of animal stories, and a short novel. Khyrunnisa is a full-time writer and lives in Thiruvananthapuram.

Bidisha Pandey

Lt Cdr Bidisha Pandey (Retd) is a former Logistics Officer in the Indian Navy with various accomplishments, including leading the Naval Marching Contingent twice during the Republic Day Parade and sailing from Cape Town to Goa as part of the Trans-Atlantic Cape to Rio International Ocean Sailing Expedition. She was awarded a Commonwealth Scholarship to pursue a Master's degree in London and now works as the Director of Trade & Investment at the Australian Trade and Investment Commission.

WHAT IS TEDx

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx. TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is called TEDxMEC, where x = independently organized TED event. At our TEDxMEC event, TED Talks video and live speakers will combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group.

ABOUT TEDx

x = independently organized event

TEDxMEC stands for the mission of TED - to propagate ideas potent enough to change the mindsets, inspire youth at large and equip them to crave a better living, beneficial for themselves and the society they dwell in. TEDxMEC features engaging talks inspiring people ranging from veteran performers and social reformers to young innovators. It serves as a stage for promoting fresh thoughts and inspiring innovation. With its inception in 2009, TEDxMEC has presented the first ever independently organized TED event in India.

ABOUT TED

TED is a nonprofit organization devoted to Ideas Worth Spreading. Started as a four-day conference in California 30 years ago, TED has grown to support its mission with multiple initiatives. The two annual TED Conferences invite the world's leading thinkers and doers to speak for 18 minutes or less. Many of these talks are then made available, free, at TED.com. TED speakers have included Bill Gates, Jane Goodall, Elizabeth Gilbert, Sir Richard Branson, Nandan Nilekani, Philippe Starck, Ngozi Okonjo-Iweala, Sal Khan and Daniel Kahneman. Follow TED on Twitter at http://twitter.com/TEDTalks, or on Facebook at http://www.facebook.com/TED.
